Study Title: Bias-Aware Skin Lesion Detection System
Purpose of the Study

You are being invited to participate in research to evaluate the fairness and effectiveness of an AI-based skin lesion detection system across different skin tones and demographic groups.

What Will Happen
  • You will upload skin lesion images for analysis
  • You will provide feedback on the system's predictions
  • You will complete questionnaires about your experience
  • You may provide demographic information (optional)
Data Collection and Privacy
  • All data will be anonymized and used only for research purposes
  • Images and personal information will be stored securely
  • You can withdraw from the study at any time
  • Data will be retained for 5 years as per university policy
